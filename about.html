About Me

Hello! I'm Jonathan George, a developer passionate about computer graphics, systems programming, and modern web technologies.

In 2025, I graduated with my Bachelors of Science in Computer Science from the University of New Hampshire. I am developing this website to showcase my projects and portfolio in hopes of getting interviews for software engineering positions.

I have more than two years of software engineering experience at NASA, where I developed and supported software for the Parker Solar Probe mission. I also worked as a Teaching Assistant for Data Structures and Algorithms at the University of New Hampshire, supporting student learning and coursework.

This website is a showcase of my projects. My latest work involves exploring WebGPU to create high-performance 3D experiences directly in the browser.

Feel free to check out my portfolio to see what I've been building!
